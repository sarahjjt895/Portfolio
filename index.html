RunWise | Run Tracking Application

Developed to provide runners a comprehensive platform for tracking, analyzing, and enhancing their performance. Integrating tracking metrics that allow the user to set their goals and provide insights on their daily, weekly and monthly accomplishments.

The Problem

Many running apps fail to fully meet the needs of avid runners, often lacking in customization, personalized feedback, and engaging features. Most current solutions provide basic tracking and goal-setting functionalities but fall short in delivering tailored insights or motivating content.


Runners are often left to settle for “good enough” rather than an app that truly enhances their training and performance.

The Solution

  • Dynamically tailored training plans that adapt based on individual performance data and progress.
  • Advanced Performance Insights that provide In-depth analysis and feedback on running form and efficiency.
  • Dynamic Goals that adjust based on user progress to keep motivation high.
  • Intuitive User Interface with seamless navigation and use across all devices.
